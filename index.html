General
• Fields marked * are required — the form cannot be submitted without them.
• Upload your quote document (PDF or Word) using the Browse button at the top.
• If you have multiple part numbers, use the + Part row button to add a row for each one.
• For tooling or mold costs, use + Tooling row. For engineering, consulting, IP, or design fees, use + NRE row.
• If your quote has a sub-assembly with a detailed BOM, upload the Excel breakdown using the BOM Excel button.
Field definitions
MOQ — Minimum order quantity. Enter the smallest quantity you will accept per order.
UOM — Unit of measure (e.g. each, lot, kg, seat/year for software).
Lead time — Time from purchase order to delivery, in weeks (e.g. "8 weeks").
Incoterms — Shipping terms that define who is responsible for freight and risk (e.g. EXW, FOB, DDP).
HTS / HS Code — Harmonized tariff code for your product. Leave blank if unknown.
COO — Country of Origin. The country where the product is manufactured.
Exceptions — Note any special conditions that differ from standard terms (e.g. minimum order value, forecast requirements).
Questions? Contact your Tenstorrent sourcing representative directly for assistance.
